Zuobai Zhang 「张作柏」
Research Scientist
NVIDIA Research, Santa Clara, CA

I am a Research Scientist in the Fundamental Generative AI Research (GenAIR) Group at NVIDIA Research, supervised by Arash Vahdat. At NVIDIA, I contribute to the Proteina generative model suite for protein structure design, and proposed Complexa, a generative protein binder design model with successful wet-lab validation. My research interests focus on developing biological foundation models, especially for proteins, from the perspectives of both representation learning and generative modeling. I also have a general interest in AI4Science and machine learning techniques, including deep generative models, geometric deep learning, and self-supervised learning.

I received my Ph.D. in Computer Science from Mila – Québec AI Institute and Université de Montréal in 2026, advised by Prof. Jian Tang. I received my B.S. in Computer Science (Honor Class) from Fudan University in 2021, advised by Prof. Zhongzhi Zhang. I was also a Machine Learning Engineering Intern at ByteDance (2020–2021), working on recommender systems.
